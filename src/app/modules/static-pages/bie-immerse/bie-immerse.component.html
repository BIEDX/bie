MEETING OVERVIEW

BIE Immerse is an annual meeting where participants get to deep-dive into recent updates and advances in breast imaging and intervention. Global experts in their respective sub-domains share their clinical and research experiences with the participants.

Course Director's Message

As a part of BIE Grand 2022 this December, we are bringing an enhanced version of our previous breast intervention workshops. The BIE Hands-on Breast Intervention workshop this year will also teach you stereo biopsy, in addition to MR-guided and US-guided biopsies. This workshop will equip you with the necessary training, tips and tricks you need to know for a successful intervention practice. Our global experts will share their years of experience with you during the sessions in an actual clinic setting. This will surely take your practice to the next level and we are pretty sure that you will enjoy the whole experience. We whole-heartely welcome you for the workshop and hope that you will be able to make the most of this opportunity. See you all soon in Singapore!

DR. NIKETA CHOTAI
