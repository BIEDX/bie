SYMPOSIUM OVERVIEW

The BIE USG and CEM Symposium 2023 is a two day lecture series designed to provide a detailed understanding of the two modalities for breast imaging from a practical standpoint. Participants will learn various concepts from basics to advanced with the help of case demonstrations.

Ultrasound (USG) Breast Symposium (Day 1) – Ultrasound breast is a basic, most widely used and cost-effective modality in breast imaging. Its role in screening and diagnostic settings is increasing but it comes with its own limitations. In this lecture series, we will learn the basics and advances in ultrasound in breast imaging. Case demonstrations are included for an enhanced understanding of concepts and a practical experience for the participants.

Contrast Enhanced Mammography (CEM) Symposium (Day 2) – Contrast enhanced mammography (CEM) is being seen as a game changer in the breast imaging armamentarium. In this series of talks on CEM by international experts, we will share the indications, practical application, newly released ACR BIRADS Lexicon, and literature review with the participants. The talks will be combined with interesting case sharing to make it an interactive and practical learning experience.

Symposium Director's Message

Over the last two years, we have received a lot of requests for USG and CEM breast education. The BIE USG & CEM Symposium 2023 is a series of talks designed to help radiologists learn the basics as well as advances in relation to the use of ultrasound in breast imaging from the comfort of your homes. You will also learn the the indications, practical application, newly released ACR BIRADS Lexicon, and literature review of Contrast Enhanced Mammography through detailed lectures from global experts and interactive discussions. This is another attempt to help you continue your education and learning in breast imaging. We welcome you all to take advantage of the opportunity and make the most of it! See you soon!
